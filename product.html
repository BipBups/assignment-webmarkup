Global story about the product

One of the biggest technology advancements in sports

While wearable tech is nothing new in the fitness and athletics world, advancements in sensor technology and accuracy have wholly revolutionized the fitness tracker industry, making wearable tech one of the biggest technology advancements in sports.

As recently as a decade ago, athletes and fitness enthusiasts interested in tracking basic workout metrics had to rely on clunky devices that were ugly and impractical. Today, wearable tech is so well-designed and functional that it’s transcended into the world of fashion.
